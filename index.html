🎴 How to Play Island Dom

Island Dom is a 4-player Bajan Cut-Throat dominoes game. Every player is on their own — no partners, no teams. First to 6 round wins takes the match.

Setup

  • Uses a standard double-six set (28 tiles).
  • Each player is dealt 7 tiles. No boneyard.
  • Whoever holds the double-six poses it to start every round.

Turn Order

  • Play moves counter-clockwise around the table — the player on your right is next.
  • On your turn, play a tile onto one of the two open ends of the chain. Your tile must match the pip value at the end you're connecting to.
  • Drag your tile from your hand onto the gold outline to place it.

Knocking

  • If you can't play any tile, tap Knock to pass.
  • If all four players knock in a row, the round is blocked.

Winning a Round

  • Domino: play your last tile. You win the round and score one.
  • Blocked: whoever has the lowest count left in their hand wins the round — but only if they are already out (have at least one prior round win). If the lowest-count player is not out yet, or multiple players tie at the lowest count, the round is scrapped and the next round restarts with the double-six.

Winning the Match

  • First player to 6 round wins takes the match.
  • If all four players have at least one win, the match is reset and starts over.

Tips

  • Play doubles early — they can box you in late.
  • Watch what opponents knock on — they don't have that pip.
  • Count your own pips. Late-game knocks decide who wins when blocked.
